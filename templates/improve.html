Why the "Improve" Phase Is the Most Important

Definition: What Is the Improve Phase?

Improve is the final phase where you identify gaps, implement lessons learned, and strengthen defenses to prevent similar incidents. Every incident makes you stronger.

Lessons = What went wrong Gaps = What was missing Actions = Fix it
Always improving

Q: What is a post-incident review?
A: Structured meeting after an incident to identify what went well, what went wrong, and how to improve.

  1. Q: What is the Improve phase for? A: To identify gaps and implement better defenses.
  2. Q: What should be done with lessons learned? A: Implement changes to prevent recurrence.
  3. Q: What is a blameless post-mortem? A: Focusing on process failures rather than individual mistakes.
  4. Q: Who leads improvement initiatives in a SOC? A: L3 analysts.
  5. Q: What does PIR stand for? A: Post-Incident Review.
  6. Q: Which SOC maturity level includes threat hunting? A: Level 3 (Proactive).
