What Happens During Investigation?

Definition: What Is Investigation?

Investigation is the deep-dive analysis of confirmed threats to understand the full scope — what happened, how, when, and what was affected. This is where you connect the dots.

IOC = Indicator Timeline = Story Scope = Impact
