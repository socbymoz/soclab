# SOC Learning Hub

## Welcome

Welcome to **SOC Learning Hub**, a cybersecurity learning platform dedicated to helping aspiring Security Operations Center (SOC) analysts develop practical blue-team skills through structured learning, hands-on labs, real-world investigations, and industry-recognized frameworks.

This platform is designed for beginners, students, cybersecurity enthusiasts, and professionals who want to build a strong foundation in security monitoring, threat detection, incident response, threat hunting, digital forensics, and SOC operations.

---

## Mission

Our mission is to provide a free and organized roadmap for learning Security Operations Center concepts and developing practical cybersecurity skills that align with real-world SOC analyst responsibilities.
